Magical Me

By: debeast591 

Description

Pretty much, Magical Me is a magic mod for DangerZone and adds some interesting magical objects...

I mostly just add what I think is interesting or funny, weather it be a multi-block structure, or it's just a simple wand, if it's interesting or funny and relates to magic, I will (probably) add it :)

Note: I'm currently rewriting Magical Me, as I noticed it was getting slightly to quantity over quality, I just added loads of things that had one use, so I'm in the process of rewriting the whole mod, so yeah. I'll try and add us much as I can that was already in the mod, but some things will be removed. Sadly, this means that it'll take more time for the first full release, but it'll be about 1000 times better, just have a little patience. I'm also working on two other mods.
